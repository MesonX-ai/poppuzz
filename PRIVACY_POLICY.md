**Last Updated: February 16, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Pop Puzzle ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Pop Puzzle" or the "App").

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Information You Provide

Pop Puzzle is designed to function entirely offline. We collect minimal information:

- **Game Progress:** Your game scores, high scores, and difficulty preferences are stored locally on your device only.
- **App Settings:** Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Network information
- Contact information

**Important:** All data remains on your device. We do not transmit any information to external servers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely to:
- Save your game progress and high scores
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide you with a personalized gaming experience

We do **NOT**:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your information
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your behavior across apps or websites
- Create user profiles

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using the device's secure storage mechanisms:
- **iOS:** Secure app container storage
- **Android:** Secure app-specific storage

### 3.2 Data Deletion

Your data is automatically deleted when you:
- Uninstall the application
- Clear app data through your device settings

### 3.3 Security Measures

While we implement appropriate security measures within the app, we cannot guarantee absolute security. However, since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, the risk of data breaches is minimal.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Pop Puzzle does **NOT** integrate with any third-party services, including:
- Advertising networks
- Analytics services
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services
- Authentication providers
- Payment processors

The app functions completely offline and standalone.

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Pop Puzzle is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

**Important Points:**
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- The app does not require account creation
- The app does not request personal data
- There are no in-app communications or social features
- The app is safe for children to use without parental supervision

Parents and guardians can review and control their children's use of the app through device-level parental controls.

## 6. International Users

Pop Puzzle is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the storage of your data on your local device in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your country of residence.

### 6.1 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:
- **Right to Access:** You can access your data stored locally on your device
- **Right to Erasure:** You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data
- **Right to Rectification:** You can modify your data through in-app actions
- **Right to Data Portability:** Not applicable as data is stored locally only
- **Right to Object:** You may discontinue use of the app at any time

## 7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA Compliance)

For California residents, under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not share your personal information for business purposes
- We do not collect personal information as defined by the CCPA
